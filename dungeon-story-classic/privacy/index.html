Dungeon Story Classic

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Dungeon Story Classic is a Puzzle RPG. Coffee Makes Games does not collect personal data from players, does not show ads, and does not use third-party tracking for advertising in the game.

What Coffee Makes Games collects

We do not collect names, email addresses, location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, camera data, advertising identifiers, or gameplay analytics from Dungeon Story Classic.

Local progress

The game may save progress on the device, such as settings, hero progress, inventory, completed quests, unlocked classes, achievements, and other gameplay state. This data is used only to keep the game working for the player.

iCloud sync

If iCloud is available on the device, the game may use Apple's iCloud services to sync save data and progress across the player's own devices. Coffee Makes Games does not receive Apple ID credentials through iCloud.

Game Center

The game may use Apple's Game Center services for achievements and leaderboards. If the player is signed in to Game Center, the game may submit achievement progress and leaderboard scores to Apple. Game Center data is associated with the player's Game Center account by Apple and may be visible to other Game Center players according to the player's Game Center settings.

Coffee Makes Games does not receive Apple ID credentials through Game Center and does not use Game Center data for advertising or third-party tracking.

Your choices

iCloud sync can be managed through the device's iCloud settings. Game Center profile, achievement, and leaderboard visibility settings are managed by Apple.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Coffee Makes Games at hello@coffeemakesgames.com.
