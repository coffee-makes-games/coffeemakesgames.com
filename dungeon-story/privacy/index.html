Dungeon Story

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

This policy explains how Dungeon Story handles diagnostics, telemetry, iCloud save sync, StoreKit purchases, and Game Center leaderboards. Dungeon Story does not include ads and does not use third-party tracking for advertising.

Diagnostics

If diagnostics sharing is enabled, the game may share technical diagnostic reports with the developers and with third-party service providers that store and process this data to help diagnose and fix bugs. Diagnostics are configured to avoid collecting any personal content, but the reports may still include app and device details, plus installation-related and issue-related technical details. If the game closed unexpectedly, it may ask whether to share pending diagnostics on the next launch. Diagnostic sharing can be turned off at any time in the options menu.

Diagnostic reports and crash reports may be processed by Sentry or similar service providers used to identify, diagnose, and fix bugs.

Telemetry

If telemetry sharing is enabled, the game may share aggregated, non-identifying gameplay statistics with the developers and with third-party service providers that store and process this data. This is used to improve the game, such as balance, pacing, and usability. Telemetry sharing can be turned off at any time in the options menu.

iCloud and CloudKit save sync

Dungeon Story may use Apple's iCloud and CloudKit services to sync game saves and progress between devices. This sync is handled through the Apple ID and iCloud account configured on the device. Coffee Makes Games does not receive Apple ID credentials or payment information through CloudKit.

StoreKit purchases

Dungeon Story may use Apple's StoreKit and the App Store to process purchases. Apple handles payment information and transaction processing. The game may store or check purchase and entitlement status so purchased content can be unlocked or restored.

Game Center leaderboards

Dungeon Story may use Apple's Game Center services to support leaderboards. If the player is signed in to Game Center, the game may submit leaderboard scores such as Best Battle Score and Total Battle Score to Apple. Game Center leaderboard data is associated with the player's Game Center account by Apple and may be visible to other Game Center players according to the player's Game Center settings.

Coffee Makes Games does not receive Apple ID credentials through Game Center and does not use Game Center data for advertising or third-party tracking.

Your choices

Diagnostic sharing and telemetry sharing can be turned off at any time in the options menu. iCloud sync can be managed through the device's iCloud settings. Purchase history, App Store account settings, and Game Center profile or leaderboard visibility settings are managed by Apple.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Coffee Makes Games at hello@coffeemakesgames.com.
